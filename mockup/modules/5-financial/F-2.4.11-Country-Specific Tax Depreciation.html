Depreciation Conventions

Half-Year Convention

50% depreciation in first and last year

  • Default for most assets
  • Applied automatically unless mid-quarter applies

Mid-Quarter Convention

Applied when >40% of assets placed in Q4

  • Check: Q4 acquisitions / Total acquisitions
  • If > 40%, mid-quarter applies to all

Full year depreciation

  • International assets
  • Mid-month option for some jurisdictions
