Accounting Standards Configuration

ASC 842 (US GAAP)

  • Finance leases: Interest expense + straight-line depreciation
  • Operating leases: Straight-line expense (single expense)
  • Short-term exemption: < 12 months recognized in P&L
  • Low-value exemption: < $5,000 recognized immediately

IFRS 16

  • All leases except short-term and low-value: ROU asset + interest
  • Interest on lease liability (separate line)
  • Depreciation of ROU asset (separate line)
  • Variable payments excluded from liability
