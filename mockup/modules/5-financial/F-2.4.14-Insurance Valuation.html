Valuation Methods

Replacement Cost

Original Cost × (Current Index / Acquisition Index)

  • Adjusts for inflation using cost indices
  • Recommended for equipment subject to price changes
  • Requires current and acquisition cost indices

Actual Cash Value (ACV)

Replacement Cost × (1 - Age-Based Depreciation)

  • Accounts for wear and age
  • Common for vehicles and electronics
  • Depreciation rate based on asset category

Functional Replacement

Cost to replace with similar functionality

  • Modern equivalent with same function
  • May be higher or lower than replacement cost
  • Useful for specialized equipment

Agreed Value

Explicitly agreed amount per policy

  • Fixed amount regardless of depreciation
  • Requires insurer agreement
  • Common for high-value unique assets
