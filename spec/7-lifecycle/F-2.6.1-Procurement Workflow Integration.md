# F-2.6.1 - Procurement Workflow Integration

**Requirement ID:** F-2.6.1  
**Module:** Asset Lifecycle Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

System shall integrate with procurement systems to support complete workflow from purchase requisition to purchase order, receiving, and asset creation.

---

## Business Rules

1. PO approval chain must be documented for SOX financial controls
2. Segregation of duties: requestor cannot approve own requisition
3. PO fully received and approved creates asset record
4. Asset acquisition cost includes PO unit price, freight, installation, and other allocated costs
5. Capitalization threshold configurable per company (default $5,000)
6. Assets below threshold are expensed, not capitalized
7. Partial PO receipts create CIP (Construction in Progress) records

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| PurchaseOrderNumber | Yes | Unique PO number (alphanumeric with dashes) |
| SupplierID | Yes | UUID of supplier |
| TotalAmount | Yes | Total PO amount (positive decimal) |
| UnitPrice | Yes | Price per unit (positive) |
| Quantity | Yes | Ordered quantity (positive) |
| CompanyCode | Yes | Company identifier |
| Status | Yes | PENDING, APPROVED, PARTIALLY_RECEIVED, FULLY_RECEIVED, CANCELLED, CLOSED |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| AssetID | System-generated UUID for created asset |
| AcquisitionCost | Calculated total cost (unit price × quantity + allocated costs) |
| PurchaseOrderID | Link to originating PO |
| CapitalizationDate | MAX of receipt date and commissioning date |

---

## Validation Rules

1. PO number must match pattern: letters, numbers, and hyphens only
2. PO number must be unique per company
3. Total amount must be positive
4. Unit price must be positive
5. Quantity must be positive
6. Supplier must exist in master data
7. PO must be in APPROVED status before asset creation
8. Received quantity cannot exceed PO quantity (without override)

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| SystemAdmin | Full access to all procurement and assets |
| ProcurementManager | Create/approve POs within authorization limits |
| AssetManager | Create assets from POs, manage asset data |
| PlantOperator | Read-only access to POs at assigned plant |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company/Plant
- **Access**: Users can only create/view POs within their organization hierarchy
- **Isolation**: CompanyCode filtering ensures company A cannot view company B POs

---

## Compliance Requirements

- SOX: PO approval authority limits enforced; requestor ≠ approver segregation
- US GAAP: ASC 360 - capitalization threshold per company policy
- ISO 55000: Asset acquisition planning aligned with procurement workflow

---

High
