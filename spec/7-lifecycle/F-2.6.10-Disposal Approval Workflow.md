# F-2.6.10 - Disposal Approval Workflow

**Requirement ID:** F-2.6.10  
**Module:** Asset Lifecycle Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Automated approval workflow based on asset value, sensitivity, and disposal method, supporting multi-level approval, parallel approval, and conditional approval.

---

## Business Rules

1. Tiered approval based on asset value: <$10K Department Manager, $10K-$100K Director, >$100K VP/CFO
2. Segregation of duties enforced: requestor cannot be approver
3. Sensitive assets require additional IT Security approval
4. Parallel approval: Finance + Technical simultaneously
5. Approval timeout auto-escalates to next level
6. Any rejection returns to requestor for revision

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| DisposalRequestID | Yes | UUID of disposal request |
| ApprovalRuleID | Yes | UUID of approval rule to apply |
| Deadline | Yes | Approval deadline (must be future) |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| ApprovalTaskID | System-generated UUID |
| ApprovalPath | Sequence of approvers |
| ApprovalRecordID | Final approval decision record |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Disposal request must be submitted before approval
2. Approval rules must be configured
3. Approver must be valid and active
4. Deadline must be a future date
5. Requestor ≠ Approver (segregation of duties)

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| SystemAdmin | Full access to approval workflow |
| DepartmentManager | Approve disposals within department limit |
| Director | Approve disposals within director limit |
| VP/CFO | Approve high-value disposals |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company/Division
- **Access**: High-value disposals require company-level approval
- **Isolation**: Approval records scoped to company

---

## Compliance Requirements

- SOX: Approval authority limits enforced; requestor ≠ approver segregation; complete approval audit trail
- US GAAP: ASC 360 - disposal approval supports financial controls
