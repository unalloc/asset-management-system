### 3.3 Business Logic

**Calculation Logic:**
```
TCO = AcquisitionCost 
    + OperatingCost 
    + MaintenanceCost 
    + DisposalCost 
    - ResidualValue

ROI = (BenefitsGenerated - TCO) / TCO * 100

AnnualCost = TCO / UsefulLife
```
