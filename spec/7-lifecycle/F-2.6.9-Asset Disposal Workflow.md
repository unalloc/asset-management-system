# F-2.6.9 - Asset Disposal Workflow

**Requirement ID:** F-2.6.9  
**Module:** Asset Lifecycle Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Manage complete process of asset disposal including retirement, sale, donation, and disassembly, supporting approval workflow and compliance checking.

---

## Business Rules

1. Asset must be in RETIRED, OBSOLETE, or SURPLUS status for disposal
2. Disposal assessment must be completed before disposal
3. Gain/Loss = Proceeds - Carrying Value (cost - accumulated depreciation)
4. Environmental compliance check required for hazardous materials
5. Data sanitization certificate required for assets with data storage
6. Approval routing based on gain/loss amount thresholds
7. WEEE-certified processor required for electronic equipment disposal
8. RCRA-compliant manifest required for hazardous waste disposal

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| AssetID | Yes | UUID of asset to dispose |
| DisposalMethod | Yes | RETIREMENT, SALE, DONATION, or DISASSEMBLY |
| Reason | Yes | Reason for disposal (max 500 chars) |
| AssessedValue | Yes | Estimated value (non-negative) |
| ExpectedProceeds | No | Expected sale/donation value |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| DisposalRecordID | System-generated UUID |
| GainLossAmount | Calculated gain or loss |
| AccountingVoucherID | Reference to financial records |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Assessed value must be non-negative
2. Expected proceeds must be non-negative
3. Asset must be eligible for disposal (not in active use)
4. Disposal assessment must be completed
5. Environmental compliance must be verified

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| SystemAdmin | Full access to all disposal operations |
| AssetManager | Initiate and manage disposals |
| Controller | Approve high-value disposals |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company/Division
- **Access**: Disposal approval at company level due to financial impact
- **Isolation**: CompanyCode filtering on disposal records

---

## Compliance Requirements

- WEEE: Electronic equipment recycling through WEEE-certified processors; recycling certificates retained
- EPA/RCRA: Hazardous waste manifest tracking; EPA Form 8700-22
- SOX: Gain/loss recognition per ASC 360; role-based approval authority
- GDPR: Data wipe certificate required before disposal (Art. 17 - Right to erasure)
- US GAAP: ASC 360 - gain/loss calculation and recognition in period of disposal

---

High
