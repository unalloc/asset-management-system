# F-2.6.3 - Capitalization Process

**Requirement ID:** F-2.6.3  
**Module:** Asset Lifecycle Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Manage conversion process from construction in progress (CIP) to fixed assets, including cost accumulation, capitalization condition judgment, and accounting voucher generation.

---

## Business Rules

1. Asset receiving must be completed before capitalization
2. CIP (Construction in Progress) amount must exist or asset in construction status
3. Accounting period must be open for capitalization
4. Asset must be "ready for intended use" before capitalization
5. Capitalization amount includes all costs: CIP accumulated cost + freight + installation + allocated costs
6. Assets below company capitalization threshold are expensed, not capitalized
7. Depreciation starts when asset is placed in service (CapitalizationDate)
8. Depreciation method and useful life assigned from asset category

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| AssetID | Yes | UUID of asset to capitalize |
| CIPProjectID | No | UUID of CIP project (if applicable) |
| CapitalizationAmount | Yes | Amount to capitalize (non-negative) |
| CapitalizationDate | Yes | Date of capitalization (must be in open period) |
| ReadinessStatus | Yes | READY or NOT_READY |
| UsefulLife | Yes | Expected useful life in years |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| CapitalizationID | System-generated UUID |
| VoucherNumber | Accounting voucher reference |
| AssetStatus | Updated to IN_USE |
| DepreciationScheduleID | UUID of created depreciation schedule |
| MonthlyDepreciationAmount | Calculated monthly depreciation |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Capitalization amount cannot be negative
2. Capitalization date must be in open accounting period
3. Asset must not already be capitalized (status ≠ IN_USE)
4. Depreciation rule must exist for asset category
5. Approval authority required for amount above user limit
6. Useful life must be assigned (> 0)
7. Amount cannot exceed CIP accumulated cost (requires override)

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| SystemAdmin | Full access to all capitalization operations |
| Controller | Approve capitalizations at company level |
| FixedAssetManager | Initiate and process capitalizations |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company
- **Access**: Capitalization executed at company level only
- **Isolation**: CompanyCode mandatory on all financial tables

---

## Compliance Requirements

- SOX: Approval authority limits enforced; preparer ≠ approver segregation
- US GAAP: ASC 360 - capitalization threshold configurable per company
- IFRS: IAS 16 - component depreciation support
- ISO 55000: Asset lifecycle transition from construction to active service
