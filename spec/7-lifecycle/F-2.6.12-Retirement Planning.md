# F-2.6.12 - Retirement Planning

**Requirement ID:** F-2.6.12  
**Module:** Asset Lifecycle Management  
**Priority:** Medium

---

## Requirement Description

Predict asset retirement time based on asset lifespan, technical obsolescence, and maintenance cost factors, develop retirement budget and replacement plan.

---

## Business Rules

1. Expected retirement date calculated as MAX of physical life, technical life, economic life
2. Retirement risk score based on: maintenance cost trend (30%), failure frequency (30%), efficiency decline (20%), compliance risk (20%)
3. Historical data (minimum 3 years) required for cost trend analysis
4. Prediction model must be configured before planning
5. Budget period must be defined for retirement planning
6. High-risk assets flagged for priority replacement

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| AssetID | Yes | UUID of asset for prediction |
| PhysicalLife | Yes | Expected physical lifespan in years |
| TechnicalLife | Yes | Expected technical obsolescence in years |
| EconomicLife | Yes | Expected economically viable lifespan in years |
| BudgetPeriod | Yes | Planning year for retirement |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| RetirementPredictionID | System-generated UUID |
| PredictedRetirementDate | Calculated retirement date |
| ConfidenceLevel | Prediction confidence percentage |
| RiskScore | Calculated retirement risk score |
| RetirementPlanID | UUID for retirement plan |
| ReplacementScheduleID | UUID for replacement schedule |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Historical data must be available for prediction
2. Prediction model must be configured
3. Budget period must be defined
4. Risk score warning threshold configurable

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| SystemAdmin | Full access to retirement planning |
| FinanceManager | Manage retirement budgets |
| AssetManager | View and update retirement predictions |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company/Division/Plant
- **Access**: Retirement budgets managed at company level
- **Isolation**: CompanyCode filtering on prediction data

---

## Compliance Requirements

- US GAAP: ASC 360 - useful life documentation; retirement budget allocation for replacements
- SOX: Retirement budget planning for financial controls
- OSHA: Safety impact assessment before retirement
- EPA: Environmental impact assessment before retirement
- ISO 55000: Lifecycle planning and value realization
