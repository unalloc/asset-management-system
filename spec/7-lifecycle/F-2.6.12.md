# F-2.6.12 Retirement Planning

## Function ID
F-2.6.12

## Function Name
Asset Retirement Planning

## Function Description
Predict asset retirement time based on asset lifespan, technical obsolescence, and maintenance cost factors, develop retirement budget and replacement plan.

## Business Rules
1. Retirement prediction model: based on physical lifespan, technical lifespan, economic lifespan
2. Multi-factor consideration: maintenance cost trend, failure frequency, energy efficiency, compliance requirements
3. Retirement budget: predict annual retired asset quantity and replacement cost
4. Replacement plan: new asset procurement time, budget allocation, old asset disposal arrangement
5. Early retirement: special circumstances (accident, technology innovation, business change)
6. Extended use: assess risk and cost of extended usage
7. Retirement impact analysis: impact on production, safety, compliance
8. Plan approval: retirement plan requires finance and business department approval

## Preconditions
- Asset historical data complete (cost, maintenance, performance)
- Retirement prediction model configured
- Budget period defined
- Replacement asset information available

## Postconditions
- Retirement plan generated
- Budget allocation suggestions
- Replacement schedule
- Approval record saved

## Input Data
1. Asset ID list
2. Historical maintenance data
3. Performance indicators
4. Technical obsolescence assessment
5. Budget constraints
6. Business requirement changes
7. Compliance requirement updates
8. New technology market information

## Output Data
1. Retirement prediction report
2. Annual retirement plan
3. Replacement budget suggestions
4. Risk analysis
5. Approval workflow
6. Plan execution tracking

## Validation Rules
1. Prediction model must be based on reliable data
2. Retirement reason must be sufficient (cost, risk, compliance)
3. Budget suggestions must comply with financial policy
4. Replacement time must consider procurement cycle
5. Plan must be reviewed by relevant stakeholders

## Error Handling
1. Insufficient data: Use simplified model and mark uncertainty
2. Prediction conflict: Manual review and decision
3. Budget exceeded: Adjust plan or apply for additional budget
4. Time conflict: Reschedule replacement time

## Priority
High
