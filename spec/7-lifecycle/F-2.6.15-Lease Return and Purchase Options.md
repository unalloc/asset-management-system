# F-2.6.15 - Lease Return and Purchase Options

**Requirement ID:** F-2.6.15  
**Module:** Asset Lifecycle Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Manage lease asset return, renewal, and purchase option execution, including return inspection, purchase price calculation, and ownership transfer.

---

## Business Rules

1. Lease must be within 90 days of expiration to trigger workflow
2. Purchase price = contract option price OR fair market value (if FMV required)
3. Return inspection creates record for damage assessment
4. ROU asset derecognized upon lease termination
5. Fair market value appraisal recommended for purchase decisions
6. Purchase budget must be approved before purchase execution
7. Renewal assessment conducted before expiration decision

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| ContractID | Yes | UUID of lease contract |
| DecisionType | Yes | PURCHASE, RETURN, or RENEW |
| DecisionDate | Yes | Date of decision |
| FairMarketValue | No | Appraisal value (if required) |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| LeaseDecisionID | System-generated UUID |
| ReturnInspectionID | UUID if returning (damage assessment) |
| PurchaseRecordID | UUID if purchasing (ownership transfer) |
| LeaseTerminationID | UUID for termination record |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Lease must be approaching expiration (within 90 days)
2. Decision must be made (PURCHASE, RETURN, or RENEW)
3. Budget must be approved for purchase decisions
4. Purchase option deadline warning issued when approaching

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| SystemAdmin | Full access to lease decisions |
| FinanceManager | Approve purchases and terminations |
| AssetManager | Coordinate return inspections |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company/Division
- **Access**: Lease decisions at company/division level
- **Isolation**: CompanyCode filtering on lease records

---

## Compliance Requirements

- IFRS 16: Right-of-use asset derecognition on lease termination; purchase option fair value; renewal term assessment; maturity analysis disclosures
- ASC 842: Same requirements as IFRS 16 for US GAAP compliance
- SOX: Lease termination accounting with audit trail
