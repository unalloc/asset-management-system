# F-2.6.13 - Asset Refurbishment and Redeployment

**Requirement ID:** F-2.6.13  
**Module:** Asset Lifecycle Management  
**Priority:** Medium

---

## Requirement Description

Manage asset refurbishment, upgrade, and modification process, redeploy refurbished assets to new location or new purpose to extend asset life.

---

## Business Rules

1. Asset must be in AVAILABLE or RETIRED status for refurbishment
2. Refurbishment budget must be approved before work begins
3. Redeployment requirements must be defined before refurbishment
4. Refurbishment cost > 60% of new asset cost triggers warning (may not be worthwhile)
5. Post-refurbishment life must be > 1.2x current remaining life to confirm benefit
6. Warranty resets upon refurbishment completion
7. Hidden damage found triggers reassessment and re-approval

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| AssetID | Yes | UUID of asset to refurbish |
| EstimatedCost | Yes | Budgeted refurbishment cost |
| EstimatedCompletion | Yes | Expected completion date |
| NewLocation | Yes | Target location for redeployment |
| NewPurpose | Yes | New use case for asset |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| RefurbishmentProjectID | System-generated UUID |
| ActualCost | Actual refurbishment cost |
| CompletionDate | Date of completion |
| QualityResult | QA outcome |
| RedeploymentRecordID | UUID for redeployment tracking |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Asset must be eligible for refurbishment (AVAILABLE or RETIRED)
2. Budget must be approved before work starts
3. Redeployment requirements must be defined
4. Cost warning threshold: 60% of new asset cost

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| SystemAdmin | Full access to refurbishment operations |
| AssetManager | Manage refurbishment projects |
| FinanceManager | Approve refurbishment budgets |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company/Division/Plant
- **Access**: Budget approval at company/division level
- **Isolation**: CompanyCode filtering on refurbishment records

---

## Compliance Requirements

- US GAAP: ASC 360 - refurbishment cost capitalization if extends life; new useful life assigned
- SOX: Refurbishment cost tracking with audit trail
- OSHA: Safety inspection required after refurbishment before redeployment
