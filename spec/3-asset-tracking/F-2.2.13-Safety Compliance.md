# F-2.2.13 - Safety Compliance

## Requirement ID
F-2.2.13

## Function Name
Safety Compliance Tracking

## Function Description
Track equipment-related safety compliance requirements including lockout-tagout (LOTO) procedures, safety inspections, violation records, and safety training association. Maintain OSHA-required documentation, track training certifications, and generate compliance reports.

## Business Purpose
Ensure equipment safety compliance to protect workers, meet regulatory requirements, and prevent workplace incidents. Provides systematic tracking of safety inspections, LOTO procedures, violations, and training certifications required by OSHA and other regulatory bodies. Reduces risk of accidents and ensures organizational compliance with safety standards.

## Core Business Rules

1. **Safety-Tracked Equipment**: Only equipment designated as safety-tracked appears in safety compliance management.

2. **Qualified Inspector**: Only inspectors with valid safety inspection certifications can perform safety inspections.

3. **LOTO Authorization**: Only technicians with LOTO authorization can apply lockout-tagout procedures.

4. **Complete Inspection Checklist**: All checklist items must be completed before inspection can be submitted.

5. **Inspection Results**: Each inspection results in PASS, FAIL, or CONDITIONAL determination.

6. **Violation Documentation**: Failed inspections or safety violations must be documented with severity level (LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, CRITICAL).

7. **Quarantine on Failure**: Equipment that fails inspection may be quarantined until corrective action is completed.

8. **Training Validation**: Personnel must have valid training certifications before operating safety-critical equipment.

9. **LOTO Before Maintenance**: Equipment requiring LOTO must have an active LOTO record before maintenance work can begin.

10. **Critical Violation Escalation**: Critical severity violations are escalated to safety manager immediately.

11. **Training Expiration Alert**: Alert when training certification is approaching expiration (30 days).

12. **LOTO Expiration**: LOTO records have defined expiration; expired LOTO triggers alert and removal.

## Data Scope

- **Creates Data:** Safety inspectors create inspection records; System generates alerts and violations
- **Views Data:** Same company/plant personnel; Safety managers have cross-plant view
- **Modifies Data:** Plant-level configuration; Safety managers handle violations and corrective actions

## Equipment Safety States

| State | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| COMPLIANT | Equipment has valid inspection and all safety requirements met |
| INSPECTION_DUE | Equipment approaching inspection due date |
| OVERDUE | Equipment past inspection due date |
| VIOLATION | Equipment has open safety violation |
| QUARANTINE | Equipment quarantined due to safety issue |

## LOTO States

| State | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| NOT_APPLIED | No LOTO currently in effect |
| APPLIED | LOTO active with energy sources isolated |
| COMPLETED | LOTO work completed, LOTO being removed |
| EXPIRED | LOTO automatically expired without completion |

## Safety Event Types

### Inspections
- Scheduled safety inspections with checklist
- Results: PASS, FAIL, or CONDITIONAL
- Failure creates violation record
- Critical failures may trigger quarantine

### LOTO (Lockout/Tagout)
- Energy isolation before maintenance
- Documents all isolation points
- Validates authorization
- Tracks application and removal times

### Incidents
- Safety incident documentation
- Linked to equipment and procedures
- Root cause analysis
- Corrective and preventive action (CAPA)

## Validation Rules (Plain Language)

1. **Equipment Safety-Tracked**: Equipment must be designated for safety tracking.

2. **Inspector Certified**: Inspector must have valid safety inspection certification.

3. **LOTO Authorization**: Technician must have LOTO authorization for LOTO operations.

4. **Checklist Complete**: All required inspection checklist items must be completed.

5. **Inspector Active Status**: Inspector must have active (not expired/revoked) certification.

6. **LOTO Isolation Complete**: All energy sources must be isolated before maintenance.

## Edge Cases

| Scenario | Expected Behavior |
|----------|-------------------|
| Equipment fails inspection | Quarantine until corrected, exception workflow |
| LOTO forgotten | Block maintenance start until LOTO applied |
| Training expired during work | Suspend operation, require retraining |
| Multiple violations | Track each separately, escalate severity |
| LOTO equipment failure | Use backup LOTO equipment, document replacement |
| LOTO expires during work | Alert, must reapply or complete work |

## Violation Severity Levels

| Severity | Description | Escalation |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| LOW | Minor issue, correct at convenience | Safety manager review |
| MEDIUM | Moderate issue, correct within timeframe | Safety manager |
| HIGH | Serious issue, urgent correction needed | Safety manager + Plant manager |
| CRITICAL | Immediate danger, stop work | Immediate escalation |
