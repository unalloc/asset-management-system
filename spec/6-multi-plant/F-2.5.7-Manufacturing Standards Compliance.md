### 1.1 Requirement Description

Ensure asset management and maintenance activities comply with manufacturing industry standards (ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, etc.). This requirement supports the multi-company hierarchy by enabling certification tracking at the appropriate organizational level, with consolidated compliance reporting across the enterprise.

## 2. Western Market Alignment Assessment

### 2.1 ISO 55000 Alignment

| Criterion | Assessment | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| **ISO 55000 Asset Management System** | Yes | Directly supports Clause 4.2 (Asset management system planning) |
| **ISO 55001 Physical Asset Management** | Yes | Supports Clause 10.2 (Continual improvement) |
| **ISO 55002 Asset Management Systems - Guidelines** | Yes | Follows certification maintenance guidelines |

**ISO 55000 Alignment Explanation:**
- ISO 9001, 14001, 45001 are integrated management system standards aligned with ISO 55000
- Certification maintenance requires documented asset management processes
- Compliance evidence supports ISO 55000 management review

### 2.2 US/EU Regulatory Compliance

| Regulation | Applies | Compliance Status | Evidence/Mapping |
|------------|---------|-------------------|------------------|
| **GDPR** (EU) | Yes | Compliant | Employee safety records protected under GDPR |
| **SOX** (US) | Yes | Compliant | Internal control documentation for compliance |
| **OSHA** (US) | Yes | Compliant | ISO 45001 aligns with OSHA requirements |
| **EPA** (US) | Yes | Compliant | ISO 14001 aligns with EPA environmental requirements |
| **WEEE Directive** (EU) | Yes | Compliant | Environmental compliance tracking |
| **GAMP 5** (Pharma) | Yes | Compliant | Quality management system for GxP environments |

**Regulatory Compliance Details:**

**ISO 9001**: Quality Management System
- Asset maintenance is a documented process requiring records
- Clause 8.5.1 requires control of production/service provision including asset fitness

**ISO 14001**: Environmental Management System
- Asset lifecycle environmental impact tracking
- Compliance with environmental permits tied to assets

**ISO 45001**: Occupational Health and Safety
- Equipment safety certification tracking
- Risk assessment documentation for assets

**GAMP 5**: For pharmaceutical manufacturing
- Computerized systems validation for asset-related systems
- Equipment qualification (IQ/OQ/PQ)

## 3. Business Rules Enhancement

### 3.1 Pre-conditions

| Pre-condition ID | Description | Validation Logic | Error Code | Error Message |
|------------------|-------------|------------------|------------|---------------|
| PRE-CMP-001 | Applicable standards identified | EXISTS(SELECT 1 FROM Standard WHERE OrganizationID = Input.OrgID) | CMP-401 | "No applicable standards defined for this organization" |
| PRE-CMP-002 | Evidence collection plan exists | EXISTS(SELECT 1 FROM EvidencePlan WHERE StandardID = Input.StandardID) | CMP-402 | "Evidence collection plan not defined" |
| PRE-CMP-003 | Internal audit plan defined | EXISTS(SELECT 1 FROM AuditPlan WHERE OrganizationID = Input.OrgID AND Status='APPROVED') | CMP-403 | "Internal audit plan not defined" |

### 3.2 Post-conditions

| Post-condition ID | Description | Success Criteria |
|-------------------|-------------|-------------------|
| POST-CMP-001 | Compliance status assessed | ComplianceScore calculated |
| POST-CMP-002 | Non-conformances tracked | NCAR count and status populated |
| POST-CMP-003 | Certification maintained | Certification expiry tracked |

### 3.3 Business Logic

**Decision Tree:**

```
START
  │
  ▼
Get applicable standards for organization
  │
  ▼
For each standard:
  │
  ├─► Evaluate compliance requirements
  │     │
  │     ▼
  │   Collect evidence
  │     │
  │     ▼
  │   Calculate compliance score
  │     │
  │     ▼
  │   Identify non-conformances
  │
  ▼
Generate compliance report
  │
  ▼
Update certification status
  │
  ▼
Schedule corrective actions if needed
```

### 3.4 Edge Cases

| Edge Case ID | Scenario | Expected Behavior |
