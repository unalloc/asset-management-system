## Overview

Multi-plant management enables enterprises to maintain centralized standards while allowing local plant autonomy. This module ensures:

- Hierarchical organizational structure from group to plant level
- Configuration inheritance and override mechanisms
- Consolidated reporting with drill-down capabilities
- Standardized classification across all plants
- Manufacturing standards compliance tracking (ISO, regulatory)
- Cross-plant asset transfer coordination
- Centralized contract management
