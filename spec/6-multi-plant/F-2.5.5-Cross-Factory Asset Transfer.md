# F-2.5.5 - Cross-Factory Asset Transfer

**Requirement ID:** F-2.5.5  
**Module:** Multi-Plant Module  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Manage asset transfer process between different factories, including cost allocation, approval workflow, and intercompany transaction handling. Ensures proper accounting treatment for asset movements between legal entities (SOX, IFRS 16) and proper tracking for WEEE/environmental compliance.

---

## Business Rules

1. Assets must be in ACTIVE or IDLE status to be transferable
2. Cross-company transfers require transfer pricing per OECD arm's length guidelines
3. Approval workflow must be completed before transfer execution
4. Intercompany invoices generated for cross-company transfers
5. Asset status changes to IN_TRANSIT during transfer, then ACTIVE upon arrival
6. Transfer pricing documentation required for SOX compliance
7. WEEE producer responsibility transfers with equipment across EU entities

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| AssetID | Yes | UUID of asset to transfer |
| SourceOrganizationID | Yes | UUID of source factory/organization |
| DestinationOrganizationID | Yes | UUID of destination factory/organization |
| TransferDate | Yes | Date of transfer (must be today or future) |
| TransferPrice | Yes | Transfer price (must be positive) |
| TransferReason | Yes | Reason for transfer (minimum 20 characters) |
| IntercompanyInvoiceID | No | UUID for cross-company invoice |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| TransferID | System-generated UUID |
| Status | PENDING, APPROVED, IN_TRANSIT, COMPLETED, CANCELLED |
| IntercompanyInvoiceID | UUID of generated invoice (cross-company only) |
| TransferRecord | Complete transfer audit trail |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Asset must exist and be in transferable status (ACTIVE or IDLE)
2. Source and destination organizations must both be active
3. Transfer date cannot be in the past
4. Transfer price must be positive (OECD arm's length)
5. Transfer reason must be at least 20 characters
6. Assets under active maintenance cannot be transferred
7. Leased assets require lessor approval for transfer
8. Encumbered assets (lien/mortgage) cannot be transferred

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| SystemAdmin | Full access to all transfers |
| PlantManager | Initiate and approve within own plant |
| AssetManager | Full access to asset transfer operations |
| Auditor | Read-only access with full scope |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company/Plant
- **Access**: Source and destination company users can view their transfers
- **Isolation**: Transfer pricing hidden from non-involved companies until approved

---

## Compliance Requirements

- SOX: Intercompany transfer approval and audit trail required
- IFRS 16: Right-of-use asset transfer accounting
- US GAAP: ASC 842 lease asset transfer
- WEEE: Producer responsibility transfers with EU equipment
- OECD: Arm's length transfer pricing guidelines

---

High
