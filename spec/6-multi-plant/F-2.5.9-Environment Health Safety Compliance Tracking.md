# F-2.5.9 - - Environment Health Safety Compliance Tracking

## Function ID
F-2.5.9

## Function Name
EHS Compliance Management

## Function Description
Track environmental, health, and safety compliance requirements, managing related inspections, training, and incident records.

## Business Rules
1. EHS regulatory requirement identification and updates
2. Safety inspection and hazard rectification
3. Environmental monitoring and emission management
4. Occupational health management and monitoring
5. EHS training records and certificates
6. Incident reporting and investigation

## Preconditions
- EHS regulatory requirements are identified
- Safety inspection plan exists
- Incident reporting procedures are defined

## Postconditions
- EHS compliance is tracked
- Safety incidents are reported and investigated
- Training compliance is maintained

## Input Data
1. EHS regulations and requirements list
2. Safety inspection plan and results
3. Environmental monitoring data and reports
4. Occupational health inspection records
5. EHS training plan and completion status
6. Incident reports and investigation results
7. Rectification measures and verification

## Output Data
1. EHS compliance status report
2. Safety inspection statistics and trends
3. Environmental monitoring reports and warnings
4. Occupational health statistical analysis
5. EHS training completion status
6. Incident statistics and analysis
7. Rectification measures tracking report

## Validation Rules
1. Regulatory requirements must be updated timely
2. Safety inspections must have comprehensive coverage
3. Environmental data must be accurate and reliable
4. Health inspections must be executed regularly
5. Incidents must be reported and investigated timely

## Error Handling
1. Regulations outdated: Return "EHS regulatory requirements not updated timely"
2. Inspection omission: Return "Safety inspection coverage incomplete"
3. Data error: Return "Environmental monitoring data error"
4. Inspection overdue: Return "Occupational health inspection overdue"
5. Report delayed: Return "Incident report delayed"

## Priority
High (for strict EHS requirement enterprises)

## Related User Stories
US-050, US-051
