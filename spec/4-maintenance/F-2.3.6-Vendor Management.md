# F-2.3.6 - Vendor Management

**Requirement ID:** F-2.3.6  
**Module:** Maintenance Management  
**Priority:** Medium

---

## Requirement Description

Manage maintenance service vendor information, qualifications, performance, and business relationships. Includes vendor basic information management, qualification certificates and validity period tracking, service classification and capability assessment, performance scoring and evaluation system, contract history and cooperation records, and vendor risk assessment.

---

## Business Rules

1. Vendor names must be unique within a company
2. Qualification status automatically updated based on certificate expiration dates
3. Expired certifications flag vendor as ineligible for new contracts
4. Expiring certifications (within 30 days) trigger renewal alerts
5. Performance score calculated from quality, timeliness, and cost factors
6. Annual risk assessment considers financial stability and safety record

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Vendor Code | Yes | Unique vendor identifier |
| Vendor Name | Yes | Name of vendor company |
| Vendor Type | Yes | Classification of vendor |
| Size | No | Small, Medium, or Large |
| Contact Information | No | Email or phone |
| Address | No | Business address |
| Active Status | Yes | Whether vendor is currently active |
| Qualifications | No | Certification details |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Vendor Profile | Complete vendor record |
| Qualification Alert | Notification for expiring certifications |
| Performance Record | Evaluated vendor metrics |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Vendor name must be unique per company
2. Certificate validity dates must be valid (end after start and after today)
3. At least one contact method required (email or phone)
4. Score must be between 1 and 5

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| Company Admin | Create and modify vendors |
| Procurement | View and manage vendor relationships |
| Division Manager | View within division |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company level
- **Access**: Vendor creation restricted to Company Admin or Procurement
- **Isolation**: Cross-company vendor access requires Group-level role |

---

## Compliance Requirements

- SOX vendor payment controls and tax documentation tracking
- OSHA contractor safety qualification
- EPA hazardous waste vendor tracking
- GAMP 5 qualified supplier evaluation

---

## Related Requirements

## Priority

Medium - Supports vendor oversight and compliance but not core maintenance operations.
