### 1.1 Requirement Description

Track and maintain key performance indicators to evaluate maintenance efficiency and effectiveness. Includes standard KPI definitions (MTBF, MTTR, maintenance cost as % of replacement value), automatic data collection and calculation, multi-dimensional analysis (by equipment, department, time), industry benchmarking and target setting, KPI trend analysis and alerting, and improvement measure tracking.

### 3.3 Business Logic

**Standard KPI Calculations:**

| KPI | Formula | Unit |
|-----|---------|------|
| MTBF | Total Operating Time / Number of Failures | Hours |
| MTTR | Total Repair Time / Number of Repairs | Hours |
| Availability | (Total Time - Downtime) / Total Time * 100 | % |
| Maintenance Cost % | (Maintenance Cost / Asset Replacement Value) * 100 | % |
| PM Compliance | (Completed PM WOs / Scheduled PM WOs) * 100 | % |
| Mean Time Between Failures | SUM(Uptime) / FailureCount | Hours |
| Mean Time To Repair | SUM(RepairDuration) / RepairCount | Hours |
| Schedule Compliance | (WOs Completed On Time / Total WOs) * 100 | % |
| Backlog Hours | SUM(OpenWO.EstimatedHours) | Hours |
