# F-2.3.13 - Calibration Schedules

**Requirement ID:** F-2.3.13  
**Module:** Maintenance Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Manage calibration schedules, execution, and certificate tracking for measurement and test equipment to ensure measurement accuracy. Includes calibration cycle management (based on usage frequency or time), calibration agency qualification management, electronic calibration certificate management, calibration result analysis and trend tracking, overdue alerts and lockout functionality, and measurement uncertainty management.

---

## Business Rules

1. Calibration status Valid when current date is before due date
2. Calibration status Expiring when within advance notice days of due date
3. Calibration status Expired triggers lockout (if enabled) when past due date
4. As-Found tolerance check: |As-Found Value - Standard| <= Tolerance
5. As-Left value is As-Found if in tolerance, otherwise Adjusted Value
6. Calibration certificates retained as electronic records with audit trail
7. Agency accreditation to ISO 17025 required for traceability

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Equipment | Yes | Measurement equipment to calibrate |
| Calibration Interval | Yes | Frequency of calibration |
| Interval Unit | Yes | Days, months, or usage-based |
| Last Calibration Date | Yes | Date of previous calibration |
| Due Date | Yes | When next calibration is required |
| Calibration Agency | Yes | External agency performing calibration |
| Tolerance | Yes | Acceptable measurement deviation |
| Uncertainty Value | No | Measurement uncertainty |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Calibration Certificate | Record of calibration with results |
| Calibration Due Alert | Notification before calibration expires |
| Equipment Lockout |Restriction when calibration overdue |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Calibration cycle must meet regulatory minimum requirements
2. Calibration agency must be qualified
3. Calibration date cannot be in the future
4. Measurement uncertainty should be recorded

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| Quality Manager | Manage schedules, upload certificates |
| Calibration Technician | Execute calibrations |
| Plant Supervisor | View within plant |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Plant level for calibration operations
- **Access**: Schedule creation by quality department only
- **Isolation**: Plant-level calibration data isolated |

---

## Compliance Requirements

- GAMP 5 / FDA 21 CFR Part 11 electronic signatures and records
- ISO 17025 traceability to national standards
- OSHA measurement equipment accuracy for safety
- Audit trail retention for 10 years
