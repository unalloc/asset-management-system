### 3.2 Business Logic

**Criticality Classification:**

| Class | Criteria | Inventory Strategy |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| Critical (A) | Equipment down = production stop; no substitute | Stock 100%, Safety stock = 2x lead time |
| Important (B) | Significant impact; substitute available | Stock 80%, Safety stock = 1.5x lead time |
| General (C) | Low impact; easy to source | Stock 50%, Reorder point only |

**Calculation Logic:**

```
Safety Stock = Z * σ * √(LeadTime + ReviewPeriod)
Where Z = service level factor (1.65 for 95%)

Reorder Point = (AverageUsage * LeadTime) + SafetyStock

EOQ = √((2 * Demand * OrderCost) / HoldingCost)
```
