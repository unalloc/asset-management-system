# F-2.9.15 - Domestic Business Practice Support

## Function ID
F-2.9.15

## Function Name
Domestic Business Practice Support

## Function Description
Support domestic business practices and workflows, including approval processes, report formats, operating habits, and business rules localization adaptation.

## Business Rules
1. Approval process: Support domestic enterprise multi-level approval, countersignature, additional signature and other processes
2. Report format: Comply with domestic enterprise common report formats and content
3. Operating habits: Interface design that matches domestic user operating habits
4. Business rules: Support domestically unique business rules and calculation logic
5. Holiday handling: Consider domestic holidays impact on business
6. Contract management: Comply with domestic contract management practices
7. Seal management: Support electronic seal and physical seal management
8. Cultural adaptation: Consider domestic business culture and communication habits

## Preconditions
- Domestic business practice research completed
- Localization requirements clear
- User has "System Configuration" permission
- Localization resources available

## Postconditions
- Approval process localization completed
- Report format adaptation completed
- Operating habit optimization completed
- Business rules configuration effective

## Input Data
1. Localization requirements list
2. Approval process template
3. Report format sample
4. Operating habit research results
5. Business rules definition
6. Holiday schedule
7. Contract template
8. Seal management requirements

## Output Data
1. Localization configuration record
2. Approval process design document
3. Report format definition
4. User interface optimization plan
5. Business rules configuration
6. Holiday handling rules
7. Contract management process
8. Cultural adaptation guide

## Validation Rules
1. Localization requirements must be reasonable
2. Approval process must match enterprise actual practice
3. Report format must meet business needs
4. Operating habits must pass user testing
5. Business rules must comply with regulatory requirements

## Error Handling
1. Configuration error: Show localization configuration conflict
2. Process error: Show approval process design problem
3. Format error: Show report format does not meet requirements
4. Rule conflict: Show business rule conflicts with system logic

## Priority
Medium

## Related User Stories
Supporting all Chinese users
