## Overview
This module supports domestic compliance requirements and localization functions, including Ministry of Finance fixed asset card format, China accounting depreciation methods, tax-accounting difference adjustment, accelerated depreciation filing management, state-owned asset management compliance, VAT invoice system integration, Golden Tax system integration, bank-enterprise direct connection integration, domestic audit requirements, Chinese interface localization, WeChat mini-program mobile counting, domestic archive management standards, domestic HR and social security system integration, China Data Security Law compliance, and domestic business practice support.
