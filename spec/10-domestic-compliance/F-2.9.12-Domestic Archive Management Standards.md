# F-2.9.12 - Domestic Archive Management Standards

## Function ID
F-2.9.12

## Function Name
Domestic Archive Management Standards Compliance

## Function Description
Comply with domestic archive management standards, including archive classification, archiving, storage, utilization, destruction and other full lifecycle management.

## Business Rules
1. Archive classification: Classify according to "Enterprise Archive Management Regulations"
2. Archiving scope: Clarify asset-related archive archiving scope
3. Archiving process: Standardized archiving process and review
4. Storage requirements: Comply with archive storage physical and environmental requirements
5. Utilization management: Archive borrowing, copying, utilization management
6. Destruction management: Archive destruction approval and supervised destruction
7. Digital management: Paper archive digitization and electronic archive management
8. Compliance check: Periodic archive management compliance inspection

## Preconditions
- Archive management standards clear
- Archive classification system established
- User has "Archive Management" permission
- Archive storage facilities available

## Postconditions
- Archive archiving completed
- Archive storage ready
- Archive utilization record established
- Compliance check passed

## Input Data
1. Archive documents (paper/electronic)
2. Archive classification information
3. Archiving attributes (confidentiality level, retention period, etc.)
4. Archiving time
5. Storage location
6. Utilization application information
7. Destruction application information
8. Digitization requirements

## Output Data
1. Archive directory
2. Archiving record
3. Storage record
4. Utilization record
5. Destruction record
6. Digitized archive
7. Compliance check report
8. Archive statistical report

## Validation Rules
1. Archive documents must be complete
2. Classification must comply with standards
3. Retention period must be reasonable
4. Confidentiality level must be accurate
5. Archiving process must be compliant

## Error Handling
1. Classification error: Show archive classification does not comply with standards
2. Archiving error: Show archiving process violation
3. Storage error: Show storage conditions do not meet requirements
4. Utilization error: Show utilization application does not comply with regulations

## Priority
Medium

## Related User Stories
Supporting compliance requirements
