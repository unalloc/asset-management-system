## Overview

Financial management provides comprehensive financial control over assets throughout their lifecycle. This module ensures:

- Multi-currency asset valuation and reporting
- Automated depreciation calculation per local standards
- Total Cost of Ownership analysis
- Tax compliance and reporting
- Insurance tracking and claims management
- Lease accounting compliance (ASC 842/IFRS 16)
