# F-2.4.10 - Lease Accounting

**Requirement ID:** F-2.4.10  
**Module:** Financial Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Handle lease assets according to ASC 842/IFRS 16 lease accounting standards. Includes finance vs operating lease classification, right-of-use asset recognition, lease liability calculation, and interest expense amortization.

---

## Business Rules

1. Finance lease test: ownership transfer, BPO, PV >= 90% of fair value, or 75% of life
2. Lease liability = PV of future payments at discount rate (IBR)
3. ROU asset = Lease liability + initial costs - incentives + dismantling costs
4. Finance lease: interest expense + straight-line depreciation
5. Operating lease (ASC 842): straight-line expense
6. Operating lease (IFRS 16): ROU asset + interest
7. Variable payments excluded from liability, recorded when incurred
8. Short-term (< 12 months) and low-value exemptions expense as incurred

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Lease Number | Yes | Unique identifier |
| Lessor | Yes | Leasing company |
| Lease Type | Yes | Finance or Operating |
| Commencement Date | Yes | When lease begins |
| Lease Term | Yes | Duration in months |
| Discount Rate (IBR) | Yes | Incremental borrowing rate |
| Payment Schedule | Yes | All lease payments |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| ROU Asset Record | Right-of-use asset with book value |
| Lease Liability Schedule | Period-by-period liability balance |
| Interest Expense | Periodic interest cost |
| Depreciation Expense | ROU asset amortization |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Lease type must be Finance or Operating
2. Discount rate must be positive
3. Lease term must be greater than 0
4. Payment schedule must have positive total
5. Lessor must be valid
6. Renewal options assessed for reasonable certainty

| Role | Permission |

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company level
- **Access**: Lease creation at company level
- **Isolation**: Cross-company leases not allowed |

## Compliance Requirements

- ASC 842 (US GAAP) lease accounting
- IFRS 16 (IFRS) lease accounting
- SOX 404 internal controls over lease accounting
- Maturity analysis and disclosure requirements
