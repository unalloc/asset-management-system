# F-2.4.11 - Country-Specific Tax Depreciation

**Requirement ID:** F-2.4.11  
**Module:** Financial Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Support country-specific tax depreciation methods such as US MACRS, ACRS, German AfA, French Amortissement, and UK Capital Allowances. Apply appropriate depreciation tables, conventions, and special treatments for each tax jurisdiction.

---

## Business Rules

1. US MACRS 200% DB: 200% declining balance with straight-line switch
2. US MACRS 150% DB: 150% declining balance with straight-line switch
3. Half-year convention: 50% first and last year
4. Mid-quarter convention: applies when >40% of assets placed in Q4
5. German AfA: apply BMF tables by property class
6. French Amortissement: apply administration tables
7. UK Capital Allowances: apply annual investment allowance rates
8. Section 179 immediate deduction up to limit
9. Bonus Depreciation applied when enacted

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Country | Yes | Tax jurisdiction |
| Tax Category | Yes | Property class for depreciation |
| Placed In Service Date | Yes | When asset was first used |
| Depreciation Method | Yes | Country-specific method |
| Convention | Yes | Half-year, mid-quarter, or full-year |
| Original Cost | Yes | Amount subject to depreciation |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Tax Depreciation Schedule | Period-by-period tax depreciation |
| Section 179 Deduction | Immediate deduction amount |
| Bonus Depreciation | Additional first-year deduction |
| Book-Tax Difference | Temporary difference tracking |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Country must be supported
2. Tax category must be valid for the country
3. Depreciation method must be valid for country and category
4. Convention must be valid for country
5. Mid-quarter check: if >40% Q4 placed, mid-quarter applies

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| System Admin | Full access |
| Tax Manager | Manage country tax rules and tables |
| Company Controller | View all |
| Country Tax Admin | Manage rules for assigned country |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Country level for rules, company level for schedules
- **Access**: Tax rules at country level, schedules at company level
- **Isolation**: Rules shared within country, schedules scoped by company |

---

## Compliance Requirements

- IRS regulations for US MACRS/ACRS
- German tax law for AfA tables
- French tax law for Amortissement
- UK HMRC regulations for Capital Allowances
- SOX 404 tax calculation controls
