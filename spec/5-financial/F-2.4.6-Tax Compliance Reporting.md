# F-2.4.6 - Tax Compliance Reporting

**Requirement ID:** F-2.4.6  
**Module:** Financial Management  
**Priority:** High

---

## Requirement Description

Generate asset-related reports compliant with tax requirements and handle tax-accounting differences. Includes tax depreciation calculations, book-tax difference tracking, deferred tax liability calculation, and tax form generation.

---

## Business Rules

1. Tax jurisdiction determines applicable depreciation rules
2. MACRS/ACRS tables for US tax depreciation
3. German AfA tables for Germany, Amortissement for France
4. Book-tax differences tracked as temporary or permanent
5. Deferred tax liability calculated on temporary differences
6. Section 179 and Bonus Depreciation applied when enacted

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Tax Jurisdiction | Yes | Country/state for tax rules |
| Tax Category | Yes | Asset classification for tax purposes |
| Tax Cost Basis | Yes | Amount subject to depreciation |
| Placed In Service Date | Yes | When asset was first used |
| Depreciation Method | Yes | Tax-specific depreciation method |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Tax Depreciation Schedule | Period-by-period tax depreciation |
| Book-Tax Difference | Temporary and permanent differences |
| Deferred Tax Liability | Calculated DTL/DTA |
| Tax Filing Data | Information for tax returns |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Tax jurisdiction must be valid and supported
2. Tax depreciation policy must be configured for the jurisdiction
3. Asset must have tax classification assigned
4. Filing period must be within valid window
5. Section 179 deduction limited to statutory maximum

---

## Access Control

| Role | Permission |
|------|------------|
| System Admin | Full access |
| Tax Manager | Create tax books, file returns |
| Company Controller | View all |
| Division Finance | View within division |

---

## Multi-Company Data Scope

- **Data Level**: Company and jurisdiction level
- **Access**: Tax filings at company level, rules at jurisdiction level
- **Isolation**: Company data isolated, jurisdiction rules shared |

---

## Compliance Requirements

- ASC 740 (US GAAP) income tax accounting
- IAS 12 (IFRS) income taxes
- IRS regulations for US tax depreciation
- EU Directives for member state tax reporting
- SOX 404 tax provision controls
