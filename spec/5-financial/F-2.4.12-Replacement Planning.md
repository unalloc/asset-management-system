# F-2.4.12 - Replacement Planning

**Requirement ID:** F-2.4.12  
**Module:** Financial Management  
**Priority:** Medium

---

## Requirement Description

Develop asset replacement plans based on lifecycle cost analysis to optimize asset investment decisions. Includes age-based and condition-based replacement triggers, NPV comparison of keep vs replace, and priority scoring.

---

## Business Rules

1. Priority Score = weighted sum of age, condition, cost, and risk factors
2. Equivalent Annual Cost (EAC) comparison: keep vs replace
3. Replace if EAC of new asset + downtime cost < EAC of current + failure risk
4. Budget integration: align replacements with available capital
5. Risk adjustment: accelerate replacement for high-risk assets
6. Optimal timing: find year with lowest lifecycle cost

---

## Input Data

| Data | Required | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Asset | Yes | Asset being evaluated for replacement |
| Replacement Cost | Yes | Cost to acquire replacement |
| New Asset Life | Yes | Expected life of replacement |
| Remaining Life | Yes | Current asset remaining useful life |
| Maintenance Cost Trend | Yes | Historical and projected maintenance costs |
| Failure Probability | Yes | Likelihood of failure |

---

## Output Data

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Replacement Analysis | Keep vs replace recommendation |
| Priority Score | Ranking of replacement needs |
| Replacement Plan | Scheduled replacements by year |
| NPV Comparison | Financial comparison of options |

---

## Validation Rules

1. Asset must be active or depreciating
2. Remaining life must be non-negative
3. Cost history required for analysis
4. Replacement cost must be positive
5. Budget constraint warnings when limits exceeded
