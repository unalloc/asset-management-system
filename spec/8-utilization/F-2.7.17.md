# F-2.7.17 Critical Asset Emergency Allocation

## Function ID
F-2.7.17

## Function Name
Emergency Allocation Process

## Function Description
Provide emergency allocation process for critical assets, support rapid response to unexpected events (production interruption, safety accident, emergency repair), minimize business impact.

## Business Rules
1. Critical asset definition: assets critical to business continuity and safety
2. Emergency scenarios: production interruption, safety accident, natural disaster, emergency repair, critical mission support
3. Fast track: simplified approval process, preset emergency approvers, automatic highest priority
4. Conflict resolution: emergency requests can interrupt non-emergency usage, compensation mechanism
5. Notification mechanism: emergency allocation auto-notifies relevant parties (safety, management, affected users)
6. Post-event handling: evaluate impact after emergency use, complete formalities, analyze improvements
7. Drill testing: periodic emergency allocation drills to ensure process effectiveness
8. Audit tracking: complete record of emergency allocation reason, process, results

## Preconditions
- Emergency scenario occurs or warning
- Critical asset definition clear
- Emergency approver available
- Notification system ready

## Postconditions
- Critical asset emergency allocation completed
- Business impact minimized
- Post-event handling initiated
- Audit record saved

## Input Data
1. Emergency scenario type
2. Critical asset requirements
3. Emergency level
4. Impact scope estimate
5. Emergency approver contact
6. Affected user list
7. Post-event handling rules
8. Audit requirements

## Output Data
1. Emergency allocation record
2. Quick approval result
3. Auto-notification record
4. Conflict resolution record
5. Post-event evaluation report
6. Improvement suggestions
7. Audit tracking report
8. Drill test results

## Validation Rules
1. Emergency scenario must be真实有效 (prevent abuse)
2. Critical asset must be actually needed
3. Emergency level must be reasonable
4. Approver must have emergency authorization
5. Post-event handling must be complete

## Error Handling
1. Abuse detection: suspected abuse triggers investigation
2. Approver unavailable: Escalate to backup approver or auto-approve
3. Asset not available: Find alternative asset or activate contingency plan
4. Process failure: Degrade to manual emergency process and improve afterward

## Priority
High
