## 2. Western Market Alignment Assessment

### 2.1 ISO 55000 Alignment

| Criterion | Assessment | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| **ISO 55000 Asset Management System** | вң“ Yes | Legal entities define asset ownership scope |
| **ISO 55001 Physical Asset Management** | вң“ Yes | Legal entity is the asset owning/responsible party |
| **ISO 55002 Asset Management Systems - Guidelines** | вң“ Yes | Supports asset management planning at entity level |

**ISO 55000 Alignment Explanation:**

Legal entities represent the primary organizational units that own and are accountable for assets under ISO 55001:2014. This requirement supports:

- **Asset management policy**: Legal entities implement group policy within their jurisdiction
- **Asset management planning**: Entity-level asset planning and budgeting
- **Support and operation**: Entity as the operational unit for asset operations
- **Performance evaluation**: Entity-level asset performance KPIs
- **Continual improvement**: Entity-level improvement initiatives
