# F-2.8.6 Inter-company Asset Leasing

## Function ID
F-2.8.6

## Function Name
Inter-company Asset Leasing

## Function Description
Manage inter-company asset leasing within the group, including lease contract management, rent calculation, expense settlement, tax treatment, and accounting treatment.

## Business Rules
1. Lease types: Operating lease, finance lease, short-term lease, long-term lease
2. Contract management: Lease contract creation, approval, execution, renewal, termination
3. Rent calculation: Based on asset value, market rent, negotiated rent, supporting fixed rent and variable rent
4. Expense settlement: Periodic rent settlement, expense allocation, internal receivable/payable reconciliation
5. Tax treatment: Rent invoice, VAT treatment, tax filing
6. Accounting treatment: Lease accounting standards (IFRS 16/ASC 842) compliant treatment
7. Asset tracking: Lease period asset location, status, usage tracking
8. Compliance requirements: Meet lease regulations and tax requirements of different countries/regions

## Preconditions
- Lessor and lessee companies clear
- Leasable asset available
- Lease contract template available
- Accounting standards configured

## Postconditions
- Lease contract effective
- Rent calculation and settlement settings
- Accounting treatment configured
- Tax filing settings

## Input Data
1. Lease asset ID
2. Lessor company ID
3. Lessee company ID
4. Lease type
5. Lease terms (period, rent, payment frequency)
6. Contract document
7. Accounting standards selection
8. Tax treatment rules

## Output Data
1. Lease contract record
2. Rent calculation table
3. Expense settlement plan
4. Accounting treatment entries
5. Tax treatment suggestions
6. Lease asset tracking record
7. Contract execution report
8. Compliance check report

## Validation Rules
1. Lessor must have asset ownership or sublease rights
2. Lease terms must be legal and compliant
3. Rent must be reasonable (consistent with market levels)
4. Accounting standards must be correctly applied
5. Tax treatment must be compliant

## Error Handling
1. Asset not leasable: Show restriction reason
2. Terms illegal: Legal department reviews and modifies
3. Rent dispute: Renegotiate or arbitrate
4. Accounting treatment error: Accounting department adjusts

## Priority
Medium
