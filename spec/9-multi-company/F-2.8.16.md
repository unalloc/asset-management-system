# F-2.8.16 Domestic Group Management Compliance

## Function ID
F-2.8.16

## Function Name
Domestic Group Compliance

## Function Description
Ensure group management complies with Chinese domestic regulatory requirements, including state-owned enterprise supervision, group reporting, related party transaction disclosure, internal control, and risk management.

## Business Rules
1. Regulation database: Chinese group management related regulations (State-owned Assets Law, Company Law, Securities Law, SASAC regulations)
2. Compliance checking: Automatically check if group management operations comply with regulatory requirements
3. Reporting requirements: Meet domestic regulatory authority reporting requirements (SASAC, CSRC, Stock Exchange)
4. Related party transactions: Related party identification, related party transaction approval, disclosure, pricing compliance
5. Internal control: Comply with domestic internal control standards requirements
6. Risk management: Comply with domestic risk management requirements (Comprehensive Risk Management Guidelines for Central Enterprises)
7. Audit requirements: Meet domestic audit requirements (internal control audit, financial audit)
8. Violation handling: Violation detection, reporting, rectification, penalty tracking

## Preconditions
- Domestic regulation database available
- Compliance checking rules defined
- Reporting templates available
- Related party data complete

## Postconditions
- Compliance checking continuously running
- Regulatory reports can be generated
- Related party transaction compliance managed
- Internal control and risk management ready

## Input Data
1. Domestic regulation content
2. Compliance checking rules
3. Reporting templates and requirements
4. Related party data
5. Internal control requirements
6. Risk management framework
7. Audit requirements
8. Violation handling process

## Output Data
1. Compliance check report
2. Regulatory reports
3. Related party transaction compliance report
4. Internal control assessment report
5. Risk management report
6. Audit preparation materials
7. Violation handling records
8. Compliance status dashboard

## Validation Rules
1. Regulation database must be latest
2. Compliance checking must be accurate
3. Reports must meet regulatory format
4. Related party transactions must be compliant
5. Internal control must be effective

## Error Handling
1. Regulation update delay: Show regulation database expiration risk
2. Compliance check false positive: Optimize checking rules
3. Report format mismatch: Adjust report template
4. Related party transaction violation: Suspend transaction until compliant

## Priority
High
