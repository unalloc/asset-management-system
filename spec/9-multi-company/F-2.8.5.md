# F-2.8.5 Cross-company Asset Transfer

## Function ID
F-2.8.5

## Function Name
Cross-company Asset Transfer

## Function Description
Manage cross-company asset transfers within the group, including transfer approval, cost allocation, tax treatment, and accounting adjustments.

## Business Rules
1. Transfer types: Paid transfer (internal sale), unpaid transfer (donation), lease transfer, temporary borrowing
2. Approval process: Transferring company approval → Receiving company approval → Group approval (if needed)
3. Pricing mechanism: Market price, cost price, negotiated price, tax assessed price
4. Tax treatment: VAT, customs duties, transfer pricing compliance, tax filing
5. Accounting treatment: Transferring company asset reduction, receiving company asset increase, internal receivable/payable accounts
6. Documentation requirements: Transfer agreement, invoice (if needed), shipping documents, acceptance certificate
7. Tracking requirements: Transfer asset history tracking (original company, current company, transfer date)
8. Compliance requirements: Meet asset transfer regulations and tax requirements of different countries/regions

## Preconditions
- Transferring and receiving companies clear
- Transfer asset can be transferred (non-restricted asset)
- Approval process defined
- Tax rules available

## Postconditions
- Asset ownership changed
- Financial adjustments made
- Tax filing triggered
- Transfer record saved

## Input Data
1. Transfer asset ID list
2. Transferring company ID
3. Receiving company ID
4. Transfer type
5. Transfer price (if applicable)
6. Transfer date
7. Approver list
8. Transfer document attachments

## Output Data
1. Asset transfer record
2. Approval status
3. Financial adjustment entries
4. Tax treatment suggestions
5. Transfer document package
6. Asset history update
7. Compliance check report
8. Internal receivable/payable reconciliation record

## Validation Rules
1. Transferring company must have asset ownership
2. Receiving company must have asset receiving capability (technical, compliance)
3. Transfer price must be reasonable (compliant with transfer pricing rules)
4. Approval process must be completed
5. Documents must be complete (according to transfer type)

## Error Handling
1. Asset not transferable: Show restriction reason (mortgaged, leased, export restricted, etc.)
2. Unreasonable pricing: Tax department reviews and adjusts
3. Approval rejected: Record rejection reason and notify relevant parties
4. Tax non-compliance: Suspend transfer until compliance resolved

## Priority
High
