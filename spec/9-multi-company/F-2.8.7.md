# F-2.8.7 Group-level Policy Templates

## Function ID
F-2.8.7

## Function Name
Group Policy Management

## Function Description
Manage group-level asset policy templates, supporting policy development, publishing, local customization, execution tracking, and compliance checking.

## Business Rules
1. Policy types: Asset procurement policy, asset classification policy, depreciation policy, disposal policy, usage policy
2. Template management: Group-level policy template creation, version management, publishing process
3. Local customization: Subsidiaries customize based on group templates (based on regulations, business differences)
4. Policy execution: Integrate policies with business processes, automatically check policy compliance
5. Exception management: Policy exception application, approval, tracking
6. Compliance reporting: Policy execution compliance report, exception report
7. Policy training: Policy training materials, training records
8. Audit readiness: Policy documents, execution evidence, exception records audit ready

## Preconditions
- Group policy requirements clear
- Policy development process defined
- Local customization rules defined
- Compliance checking mechanism available

## Postconditions
- Group policy template published
- Subsidiary localization completed
- Policy compliance checking integrated
- Audit materials prepared

## Input Data
1. Policy type
2. Policy content (text, rules, parameters)
3. Applicable scope (company list, asset type list)
4. Local customization rules
5. Exception approval process
6. Compliance checking rules
7. Training materials
8. Audit requirements

## Output Data
1. Group policy template
2. Subsidiary localized policy
3. Policy compliance check results
4. Exception approval records
5. Compliance report
6. Training records
7. Audit materials
8. Policy execution dashboard

## Validation Rules
1. Policy content must be legal and compliant
2. Applicable scope must be clear
3. Local customization cannot violate group core principles
4. Compliance checking rules must be executable
5. Exceptions must be based on legitimate reasons

## Error Handling
1. Policy conflict: Detect and resolve policy conflicts
2. Over-customization: Group review and approve local customization
3. Compliance check failed: Show violations and suggest correction
4. Exception abuse: Monitor exception frequency and patterns to prevent abuse

## Priority
High
