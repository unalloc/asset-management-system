# Target Users

## User Roles

### 1. Asset Managers
**Global/Regional/Local Asset Managers**
- Register and categorize new assets
- Manage asset information and documentation
- Coordinate asset transfers between locations
- Monitor asset status and utilization
- Generate asset reports

### 2. Finance Department
**Financial Analysts and Accountants**
- Track asset values in multiple currencies
- Manage depreciation calculations
- Prepare tax compliance reports
- Analyze asset-related expenses
- Support budget planning and forecasting

### 3. IT Department
**IT Asset Managers**
- Manage IT equipment lifecycle
- Track software licenses
- Handle IT asset refresh cycles
- Support security compliance

### 4. Operations Managers
**Plant and Production Managers**
- Monitor equipment performance and OEE
- Coordinate maintenance activities
- Manage asset allocation within operations
- Track production metrics

### 5. Maintenance Teams
**Maintenance Managers and Technicians**
- Schedule and execute preventive maintenance
- Manage work orders and repair tasks
- Track spare parts usage
- Document maintenance history
- Coordinate with vendors and service providers

### 6. Procurement Teams
**Procurement Officers**
- Manage asset procurement workflows
- Coordinate with suppliers
- Track purchase orders and deliveries
- Handle asset receiving and acceptance

### 7. Compliance Officers
**Quality and EHS Managers**
- Monitor regulatory compliance
- Manage certifications and audits
- Track environmental requirements
- Ensure safety compliance

### 8. Executive Leadership
**Group and Company Management**
- View consolidated reports
- Monitor group-wide asset performance
- Make strategic asset decisions
- Review compliance status
